About Me

Hi! I'm Patrick Lyons, a third-year student at Limerick Institute of Technology, pursuing a BSc. in Internet Systems Development. I have a strong passion for the online payment and banking sectors and aim to gain as much experience in these fields as I can during my studies. My long-term goal is to continue my education with a master's degree in Computer Science.

I have a wide range of technical skills, including proficiency in Java, JSP, SQL, JavaScript, HTML, PHP, CSS, and React, as well as familiarity with XML. I have experience working with databases, programming software, and software testing, and I am certified in Microsoft Office. Additionally, I have experience working in an agile team environment, using scrum methodology and git.

During my summer internship at Fiserv (Nenagh office), I was part of the cloud-poi team and took the lead on testing the first roll-out of translations for payment terminals. I was also able to reduce technical debt and became proficient with GitLab. My previous work experience at Moylan's bar has honed my customer service and cash handling skills, as well as my ability to work efficiently in fast-paced environments.

I am eager to bring my skills and experience to a new opportunity and am available for a 7-month internship starting from March. If you're interested in learning more about me, please feel free to reach out via my contact page or LinkedIn.
